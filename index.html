Creative Commons Digital Asset Finder

Overview

The Creative Commons Digital Asset Finder is a web application that enables users to search Wikimedia Commons and explore media with associated rights and metadata.

Developers, designers, and researchers can quickly locate images and other media and view structured metadata aligned with common digital library standards.
